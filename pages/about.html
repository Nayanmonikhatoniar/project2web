Our Story

2015

Founded

Animal Welfare People was founded by a group of animal lovers in Dibrugarh who wanted to make a difference in the lives of street animals.

2016

First Shelter

Established our first small shelter facility with capacity for 20 dogs and 10 cats.

2018

Registered NGO

Officially registered as a non-profit organization under the Societies Registration Act.

2020

Expanded Operations

Moved to a larger facility and expanded our services to cover all of Dibrugarh district.

2023

Present Day

Now rescuing over 500 animals annually with a team of 15 dedicated staff and 50+ volunteers.

Our Mission

To reduce animal suffering in Dibrugarh through direct rescue efforts, medical care, community education, and promoting humane treatment of all animals.

Our Vision

A society where all animals are treated with compassion and respect, and where no animal suffers from neglect or abuse.
